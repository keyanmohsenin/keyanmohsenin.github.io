# Pixelberry Studios

## Choices: Stories You Play
Choices: Stories You Play is a mobile interactive fiction platform. I was narrative lead on The Freshman, The Haunting of Braidwood Manor, and Hero.
<br />

![](../../assets/images/choices-covers.png)
### The Freshman
The Freshman was one of three launch titles for Choices: Stories You Play and its most successful. I developed the world and characters of the game, outlined the major story beats, and wrote the chapters featuring a romance arc with Kaitlyn, one of the game's three possible love interests. I also co-wrote the bookending chapters of the game along with the other members of my team.

The template set by The Freshman was followed in subsequent Choices releases and has spawned countless imitators on the App Store. Aaron A. Reed, a game designer and academic, explored the impact of The Freshman and Choices on the mobile interactive storytelling market in his chronological look at interactive fiction, [50 Years of Text Games](https://if50.substack.com/p/2016-the-freshman).

### The Haunting of Braidwood Manor
The Haunting of Braidwood Manor was a short, experimental foray into Gothic horror. I developed the world and characters, outlined the major story beats, and wrote all of the game's six chapters.

**Featured Fan Art:** [MC being called out by her brother's ghost](https://www.tumblr.com/brchoicesbr/641310093838827520/because-of-you-i-cant-find-peace-i-didnt-do) by Tumblr user brchoicesbr

### Hero
Hero was another experiment, this time blending the established Choices formula with the superhero genre. I developed the world and characters, outlined the major story beats, and wrote the majority of the game's fifteen chapters.

**Featured Fan Art:** [Eva aka "Minuet"](https://www.tumblr.com/lights-fanart/182576110180/eva-aka-minuet-from-hero-fan-art-from-fall) and [Kenji aka "Talos"](https://www.tumblr.com/lights-fanart/182576127180/kenji-katsaros-aka-talos-from-hero-fan-art) by Tumblr user lights-fanart

## High School Story / Hollywood U: Rising Stars
![](../../assets/images/hss-hwu-icons.png)
I joined the studio just after the launch of High School Story as a junior writer and helped launch Hollywood U a year later.

Both games involve a light simulation layer (building a school and unlocking new characters) coupled with ongoing narrative quests. I had the opportunity to write nearly every type of content required by these games, including main story quests, secondary quests, premium quests, outfit descriptions, building descriptions, and weekly polls. I particularly enjoyed pitching new premium characters and writing their associated quests—some of my favorites include the ghostly [Penelope Waldorf](https://playhollywoodu.fandom.com/wiki/Penelope_Waldorf) and [Rok the Reaver](https://playhollywoodu.fandom.com/wiki/Rok), a method actor who never breaks character.

**Note:** These games have been officially sunset by Pixelberry Studios and are no longer available to download.
